About Us

Paws and Claws is a pet care center that provides services to pet owners, offering peace of mind when they are away on holiday or at work. We provide various services such as clinical care, grooming, and overall pet care.

Founded by Managing Director Ajin M, Paws and Claws has grown into a trusted service that is run by a team of dedicated and professional staff. Our franchise recruitment process ensures that only the most caring and qualified individuals join the team.

All of our dog walkers and pet sitters are police checked, fully insured, and trained in pet first aid. Our center is registered with the Pet Industry Federation, promoting excellence in the pet care industry across India.

Our doctors and staff are friendly, compassionate, and dedicated to the well-being of your pets. We take great pride in the professional capabilities of our team, who are carefully selected for both their skills and their love of animals.
